Responsibilities & Scope

  1. Professional Ethics: Coach agrees to follow the Ethics and Standards of Conduct set forth by the International Coach Federation (ICF). The ICF's Code of Ethics is outlined in detail at coachingfederation.org/ethics/code-of-ethics.
  2. Client Ownership: The Client's emotional, mental, and physical well-being is entirely their own responsibility. Likewise, the Client is responsible for all choices, decisions, behaviors, and actions. The Coach is not liable or accountable for any action or inaction, as well as for any direct or indirect results of service provided to the Client.
  3. Non-Therapeutic Nature: It is critical for the Client to understand that coaching does not substitute for therapy, psychotherapeutic clinical support, or mental health treatment. It is in no way designed to treat any psychological disorder, clinical condition, or clinical medical problem, nor does it prevent them.
  4. Medical & Longevity Disclaimer: IMPORTANT HEALTHSPAN & BIOMETRIC DISCLAIMER: Kristian Sokol is a specialized Life & Longevity Coach. He is not a licensed clinical medical doctor, registered dietitian, or pharmacologist. Any protocol adjustments, analyses of wearable data (such as sleep metrics, heart rate variability, biomarkers), or supplement frameworks are for informational, healthspan optimization, and lifestyle design purposes only. They do not constitute diagnostic medical advice or physician treatment plans. The Client must consult their General Practitioner (GP) or appropriate clinical specialist before altering physical regimes, medical supplements, or nutritional profiles.
  5. Professional Insurance: The Coach maintains comprehensive professional indemnity insurance coverage for both life coaching and longevity coaching practices.
  6. Commitment to Honesty: A successful coaching dynamic relies heavily on truthfulness and open dialogue. To ensure optimal progress, the Client agrees to communicate truthfully, engage in weekly active accountability tracking, and share direct feedback.

Coaching Package, Arrangements & Access

Both parties agree to establish the following program arrangements:

  • Selected Package: 4-Week Kickstart Program
  • Format & Cadence: One structured weekly coaching session lasting 45 to 60 minutes. Meetings are held via Zoom, Skype, telephone, or mutually coordinated face-to-face sessions.
  • Between-Session Support: The Coach will provide continuous asynchronous support to track habit metrics, nutrition logs, or biometric trends. The Client can access the Coach via email, text, or WhatsApp. The Coach will respond within a maximum of 24 hours. This between-session administrative support is protected at a strict 30-minute block per week per client to maintain executive quality.

Rescheduling, Cancellations & Expirations

  • 24-Hour Notice Window: The Client must provide at least 24 hours notice in advance if they wish to reschedule a coaching session. Rescheduled sessions will be made up within the course of the ongoing package.
  • Short-Notice Forfeiture: No replacements, credit roll-overs, or makeup sessions are possible if cancellations are requested with less than 24 hours notice.
  • Non-Refundable Commitment: Coaching requires a full investment of physical and mindset energy. Refunds are not generally offered, ensuring both parties respect the commitment threshold. Exceptional, sudden medical circumstances are reviewed strictly case-by-case.
