The Toolkit

A small collection of the exercises and calculators I use with clients. Pick a category, choose a tool, work through it at your own pace. Nothing here is saved, so feel free to explore.

Wheel of Life
See where your life feels balanced, and where it doesn't
›

Rate your current satisfaction in each of these 8 areas from 1 (needs real attention) to 10 (couldn't be better). Be honest, not aspirational. This isn't about where you want to be, it's a snapshot of where you actually are right now. Once you've rated all 8, your wheel will show you the shape of your life at a glance.

1 — Needs attention10 — Couldn't be better

This is a reflection tool, not a diagnostic. Your results reset once you leave or refresh this page.

Limiting Belief Rewrite
Catch the stories holding you back, and rewrite them
›

Every loop starts with a story you've told yourself so many times it feels like fact. Write down 5 of those "negative" beliefs, honestly, then sit with each one and write the true, empowering belief you'd rather run on instead. Don't overthink it, write what you know deep down is actually true.

This is a reflection tool, not a diagnostic. Your results reset once you leave or refresh this page.

BMI Calculator
A quick, honest starting point for your health baseline
›

BMI is a blunt instrument, it doesn't know the difference between muscle and fat, and it's not the whole picture of health. But it's a useful, fast starting point before we dig into what actually matters: body composition, energy, and how you function day to day.

--
--

BMI is a general guide only and not a substitute for medical or clinical advice.

Sleep & Recovery Score
How well your body is actually recovering
›

Longevity isn't built in the gym, it's built while you sleep. This quick check looks at the five things that shape real recovery: how long you sleep, how broken that sleep is, and how your energy actually holds up the next day.

--
--

This is a self-reflection score, not a medical sleep assessment.

Wheel of Business
Find the part of your business quietly holding everything else back
›

Same idea as the Wheel of Life, aimed at your business. Rate each area honestly from 1 (barely functioning) to 10 (genuinely strong). Most businesses don't fail from one big mistake, they stall because one or two areas quietly fall behind while everything else compensates. This shows you where.

1 — Barely functioning10 — Genuinely strong

This is a reflection tool, not a diagnostic. Your results reset once you leave or refresh this page.
